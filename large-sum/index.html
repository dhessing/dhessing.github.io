Large sum

Mar 30, 2016 • in Project Euler • Problem 13

Work out the first ten digits of the sum of the following one-hundred 50-digit numbers.

Not much to say. Assuming we save the above as a list called numbers, just do:

(->> (apply + numbers)
     (str)
     (take 10)
     (apply str))

And clojure’s BigInt will do the rest.

Done.
